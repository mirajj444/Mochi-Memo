Our Story

Designed with care, made to inspire

Behind the paper

Mochi Memo began as a passion project in a tiny apartment, where our founder would handcraft notebooks and journals for friends. What started with simple paper covers and hand-stitched bindings has grown into a beloved stationery brand known for its whimsical designs and exceptional quality.

Today, our team of 14 stationery enthusiasts continue to design and produce products with the same care and attention to detail that started it all.

Our Mission

At Mochi Memo, we believe stationery should spark joy and creativity. We're committed to:

  • Using sustainable materials in all our products
  • Supporting independent artists through collaborations
  • Creating designs that make you smile
  • Reducing our environmental footprint with every step
